[[Safford, Graham County, Arizona Territory|Safford]], Graham Co. A.T.
March 5 [18]98

President Wilford Woodruff
[[Great Salt Lake City, Great Salt Lake County, Utah Territory|Salt Lake City]]

Dear Brother I 
am in receipt of a letter from you 
notifying me that I have been 
suggested and accepted to take a 
mission to the southern States. I
except the call and will be on hand 
and ready to leave Salt Lake City on
the day named in your letter

Your Brother in the gospel
[[William Claudius Packer|Wm. C. Packer]]

[[James Robert Welker|James R Welker]] Bishop
