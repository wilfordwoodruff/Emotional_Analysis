At a meeting of the Board of Directors of the Chamber of Commerce, held on Thurs- day, December 3rd, the following resolutions were unanimously adopted:  Resolved, That it is the sense of this organization to address a petition to the members of the Legislature of Utah, for the purpose of impressing upon that honorable body the necessity to appropriate a sufficient sum from the revenues of this Territory in order that the great resources of its mines, its agriculture, horticulture, manufacturing industries, and live stock, be properly represented at the World's Columbian Fair.
