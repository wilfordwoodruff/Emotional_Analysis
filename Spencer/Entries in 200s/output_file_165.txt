The Twelve Apostles hold the same keys and  authority to preside over the whole Church in all the world  in the Absens of the first Presidency. The seventies are special Messengers to kingdom of God in all the world under the direction of the Twelve and to preside in the Abscens of the first Presidency and Twelve. A Patraiarch may also be ordained to be A Patriarch to the whole Church A Presiding Bishop may also be ordained to preside over the lesser Priesthood and Bishopprick in all the world But all other Presidencies are Local and belong to the several  Stakes of Zion. A Presiding High Priest Presides over  the High Priest of that stake of Zion in which He resides An Elder Presides over 96 Elders, A Priest over 48  Priests A Deacon over  12 Deacons and all are local officers    ENDENTRY October 30, 1876 ~ Monday   Oct 30.
