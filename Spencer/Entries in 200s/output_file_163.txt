Woodruff    ENDENTRY August 30, 1875       and the drowning of W FIGURE Harris having shot himself in the Head distance 25 Miles   ENDENTRY Aug 30, 1875 we took cars at Salt Lake  City in the Evening 50 Miles    of Men &amp; boys Hunting an old she griserly bear &amp; her two  cubs they had killed quite a Number of sheep, the  Man had got the two cubs would weigh about 100  each &amp; badly wounded the old one with several balls &amp; One leg broaken they probably got,  met another man who had killed a cinamon bear    United  Order at his Office Elder G Q Cannon and myself called upon Brother George Albert Smith and found him vary low we laid hands upon him Administered to him and blessed for the last time in this life FIGURE    ENDENTRY August 31, 1875    Met in the old Tabernacle at 10 oclok, being the first  Presidency, Twelve Apostles, &amp; some fifty others without  wives W Woodruff was Mouth in prayer President Young had the Minutes read was excepted the Rules were then read twice and remarks made  by Elder John Taylor followed by an Address from President Brigham Young He said I am the President of  the Twelve Apostles &amp; the Lord has Never acknowledged any other man as the President of the Twelve Except myself ownly what was said about Thomas B. Marsh and it is the duty of the Twelve to know the mind and will of God concerning them and their duties as much as for me to know it our wives were received into the order and children   ENDENTRY  George Albert Smith born June 26, 1817  Died Sept. 1, 1875.  Aged 58 years, 2 Months &amp; 5 dayes Blessed are the dead wh in the Lord    ENDENTRY
