ENDENTRY Aprail 29, 1883 The following incident of History wars repeated to W Woodruff  On the Evening of Aprail 29, 1883 By Wm Davis 88 years of age and Blind. He said After they were driven from Far West and Liberty Jail He Wm Davis went to Liberty to see the prophet while there Mr Weathers the County Surveyor came to him and said Mr Davis I want to ask you some questions and I want you to tell the truth will you do it. The following names  were then cut off from the Church for various crimes   ENDENTRY July 18, 1883 ~ Wednesday   July 18, 1883  FIGURES I receivd 3 letters to day from Ray and Gardo House in the forenoon on Land Matters. The Central Pacific Rail Road Company offered to sell to the Mormons all there lands in Utah Territory amounting to some  1,200000 Acres for about $1,000000 But on investigation it was shone that nine tenths of it was Mountain  and Desert and could not be cultivated so the offer was Declined I Attended council in the Afternoon    ENDENTRY July 19, 1883 ~ Thursday   19 FIGURE I wrote 3 Letters to M S Ray and Charles A Foster    ENDENTRY July 20, 1883 ~ Friday   20 I spent the day in the office I wrote 1 Letter to FIGURE C. S. Christianson I attended an Exhibition of paintings of Charles Hancock representing the History of the Church in the Cannon  myself, the High Council and seven others were present it was  quite a course Painting but I think laid a foundation for a correct History of Events as far as it went    ENDENTRY July 21, 1883 ~ Saturday   21. I spent the day in the Sarah  FIGURE I took cars at 3 oclok and rode to J F Smith  and A. H. Cannon we held a priesthood Meeting in the Evening of the seventies Edward Stephenson spoke a short time followed By Abram H. Cannon on the organization of the seventies. W Woodruff spoke 15 Minutes on the Priesthood I spent the night at Br Paxmans distance of the day 95 Miles    ENDENTRY July 22, 1883 ~ Sunday   22 Sunday I Attended the Sabbath School Conference in the Morning in the Meeting House at 10 oclok Prayer By G Kendal we heard the verball report of the Superintendents   ENDENTRY Dec 25 1883 to the office of a seventy I think the first Lamanite  Ever ordained to that office in this dispensation There was 316 Baptisms to day which is called Christmass. Amas Hawks  to the office of Seventy. I dined with with Br Snow I attended a wedding Party at Sister Nixons had suppair in a crouded House I returned to my  FIGURE room and wrote to A F McDonald    ENDENTRY December 26, 1883 ~ Wednesday   26 I spent the forenoon in the A Hinkley FIGURE and advised him not to go to Sonora but to do what good he could whare He was    ENDENTRY December 27, 1883 ~ Thursday   27 I went to the Joseph Smith Liston and Elizabeth A Riding at the Altar in the Marriage  covenant then rode with D H Cannon  to the sugar loaf and clum up a 10 foot perpendicular  rock took a view of the city then rode to the red stone  quiry also the Black stone quiry I went to the Temple and sent Letters to Ray I spent the Evening with Br Thompson and family 16 Miles    ENDENTRY December 28, 1883 ~ Friday   28 I called upon Br Wakefield then went to the Temple and in sercarching my record I find I  have been baptized for 2934 of my Dead have Endowments for 2305 and have 628 more to be Endowed for I have been sealed for 978 couple  of Dead friends I have been sealed for 134 couple of  dead friends on this present visit. There was 57  Endowments to day 30 ordinations and 31 sealings I  dined with Br McAllister   ENDENTRY March 8, 1884 ~ Saturday   March 8, 1884 FIGURE Phebe W. Woodruff is 77 years old today This is Phebe Whitemore Carter Woodruffs Birth day She is 77 years old this day at 10 oclok A.M. we have both been preserved in a wonderful manner through all the vicissitudes of this life so far and both are still in good reasonable health.  I took cars and rode to Woods Cross to attend a quarterly conference at Bountiful we Met at 10 oclok Peter Barton Prayed The Statistical Report was read Davis stake contained 2 Patriarch, 136 High Priest 220 seventies, 326 Elders, 75 Priests, 70 Teachers, 108 Deacons 3810 Officers and Members, 1586 Children under 8 years 5396 Total of Souls. Four Bishops then made a verbal report of their wards. Then W Woodruff spoke 50 Minuts upon the organization of the Priesthood and its labor we dined with Br Call, and Met again at 2 oclok Joseph F Smith spoke 60 Minuts I spent the night at Br Calls 10 Miles    ENDENTRY March 9, 1884 ~ Sunday   9 Sunday we Met at 10 oclok G Q Cannon having arived He spoke 63 Minutes followed By W Woodruff 15 Minutes. I dined with Phebe Sessions and Met in the Afternoon at 2 oclok Prayer By Wm R Smith spoke 12 Minutes Anson Call 7 Minutes, Samuel W Richards Joseph F Smith 35 G Q Cannon 15, W Woodruff 5 which closed the conference.
